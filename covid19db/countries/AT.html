Overview of measures by country

Austria

Eurofound has identified 35 measures in Austria in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Measures to prevent social hardship with 7 cases (20%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with the next main update scheduled for July 2020.

This overview was prepared by Bernadette Allinger (Forba) and Eurofound.
