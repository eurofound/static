      COVID-19: Policy responses across Europe

Overview of measures by country

Austria

Eurofound has identified 64 measures in Austria in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Protection of workers, adaptation of workplace with 13 cases (20%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, and data are updated in regular intervals.

This overview was prepared by Bernadette Allinger (Forba) and Eurofound.
