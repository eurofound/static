Overview of measures by country

Germany

Eurofound has identified 23 measures in Germany in the context of the COVID-19 pandemic. Most cases fall into the category Supporting businesses to stay afloat with 11 cases (48%). The data collection to date started in March 2020, with the next main update scheduled for July 2020.

This overview was prepared by Birgit Kraemer (Hans Boeckler Foundation), and Eurofound.
